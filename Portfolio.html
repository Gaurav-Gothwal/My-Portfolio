Internship

Biomedical Instumentation

Inter at IIT Mandi

During my internship at IIT Mandi iHub HCL Foundation, I had the privilege of delving into the captivating realm of biomedical instrumentation, a field that blends technology with healthcare innovation. My primary focus was on devising methodologies to enhance the affordability and accuracy of ECG (Electrocardiogram) devices. With a goal to make ECG technology more accessible, I embarked on a journey to reduce the cost while maintaining or even enhancing result accuracy. To achieve this, I employed a multidimensional approach. Leveraging my technical expertise, I harnessed the power of cutting-edge tools and devices. To measure and analyze heart rate variability, I incorporated the use of the Polar H10 chest belt and the emWave device. These instruments provided valuable data points that were crucial in understanding cardiac patterns and deviations. I further integrated Arduino sensors to capture physiological signals, expanding the scope of my data collection and analysis. In my pursuit of refining ECG accuracy, I utilized advanced software like Kubios. This software enabled me to process and interpret the collected data, extracting insights that would contribute to the enhancement of the overall accuracy of the ECG devices. By analyzing the data with precision, I aimed to minimize errors and anomalies that could potentially affect the reliability of the results. Throughout this engaging project, I meticulously collected and analyzed over 20 samples from various individuals. These samples formed the backbone of my research, offering a comprehensive view of the effectiveness of the methods employed. Through rigorous testing, I sought to validate the impact of the techniques on both cost reduction and result accuracy. Ultimately, my internship experience at IIT Mandi iHub HCL Foundation provided me with invaluable insights into the convergence of technology and healthcare. The project underscored the significance of interdisciplinary collaboration in addressing real-world challenges, and it reinforced my commitment to creating meaningful solutions that can impact lives positively.
